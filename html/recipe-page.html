Thin Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 Tbs sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1½ cup milk

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix the eggs, salt and sugar.
  2. Add flour and milk, mix together until the batter is smooth.
  3. Heat up a frying pan on a medium-high heat.
  4. Place butter (or oil) on the pan, pour on batter and spread it to cover the whole pan.
  5. Cook until the bottom turns light brown, flip over and repeat on the other side.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
